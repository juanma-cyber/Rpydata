Global temperature project API-made in python(Spider)

Real-time temperature tracking through a 3D graph created with Python is a crucial tool in various fields, including meteorology, environmental science, and industrial processes. This method allows for the visualization of temperature changes over time, providing valuable insights into trends, patterns, and anomalies. In meteorology, real-time temperature tracking helps forecasters monitor weather conditions and predict future trends. It enables them to identify temperature fluctuations that may indicate the onset of severe weather events, such as heatwaves, cold snaps, or storms. This information is vital for issuing timely warnings and ensuring public safety.
